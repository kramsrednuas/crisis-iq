The Problem

Your Firm Is Not Ready

When a crisis hits — an SEC investigation, a market disruption, a key executive departure, a regulatory action — your client-facing teams are the last to know and the first to get the call.

01

No Approved Guidance

Relationship managers and advisors improvise under pressure. Legal sends a press statement written for external media — not a script for the client asking "Is my money safe?" right now.

02

Inconsistent Messaging

A junior associate says something different than a branch manager, who says something different than a senior advisor. Every variation is a liability — regulatory, legal, and reputational.

03

No Audit Trail

When regulators ask what your employees communicated to clients — and when — you have emails, Slack threads, and call logs. Not the documented compliance record they expect to see.

04

Tools Built for Other Problems

Everbridge handles active shooters. Poppulo manages internal newsletters. Teams facilitates conversation. None were built for the regulatory and reputational crises financial institutions actually face.
